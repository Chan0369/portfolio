Doughnuts! Quote

This motion video is inspired by the quote that you might have heard of by many doughnut lovers. It is true, I'm a huge fan of tree nuts epsically peanuts. But I do really like to eat doughnuts, so I was craving doughnuts one day so I created started doodling and made the quote.

Process

This project started with the sketech of the quote that I mentioned before. Then created in the video in Adobe After Effects. After the planing and figuring out the scenes of course. The illustration that was used is form the sketech that I did while I sketeched the qoute.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was the timing. It was hard to get the perfect timing to have the falling doughnuts flow nicely down the page and have it fall naturally.
